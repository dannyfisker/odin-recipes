Chocolate cake

Chocolate cake in a baking pan is a simple, but also incredibly delicious cake recipe.

Below you will find the best recipe for the spongy and airy chocolate cake.

Equipment

  • Roasting pan

Ingredients

Chocolate cake

  • 250 g. Wheat flour
  • 300 g. Sugar
  • 6 tbsp. Cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp. Natron
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 pieces of Egg
  • 150 g. Softened butter
  • 2Â½ dl. Water

Glaze

  • 200 g caster sugar
  • 3 tbsp. cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp. Butter
  • 30-40 ml. Milk

Steps for making chocolate cake

  1. Turn on the oven at 170 degrees fan.
  2. Beat softened butter and sugar with a hand mixer until white and foamy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time so that they are mixed well.
  4. Add the other ingredients and stir with a hand mixer for 2-3 minutes until the dough is completely smooth.
  5. Pour the chocolate cake batter into an ovenproof dish or small baking pan, greased or lined with baking paper.
  6. Bake the chocolate cake on the middle rack in the oven for approx. 35 minutes.
  7. When the cake is finished baking, it must be cooled.

Steps for making the glaze

  1. Glaze: Cocoa, icing sugar, soft butter and lukewarm milk are mixed so that it is thick and spread on when the cake is cold.
