Swedish meatballs with mashed potatoes

Spaghetti and meatballs is an swedish dish consisting of mashed potatoes, brown sauce and swedish meatballs.

Following ingredients is what makes this recipe successful and tasteful.

Ingredients

Swedish meatballs for 4 people

Swedish meatballs

  • 0.5 dl breadcrumbs
  • 1 dl milk
  • 1.5 teaspoons of salt
  • 450 g minced pork/veal or pork/beef
  • An egg

Brown sauce

  • 25 g butter for frying
  • 500 ml cooking cream, I use 18%
  • beef bouillon cube
  • Brown sauce color (Kul√∏r)
  • a tsp. currant jelly
  • cornstarch
  • salt and pepper
  • Served with mashed potatoes

Steps for making swedish meatballs

  1. Mix breadcrumbs well with salt and add milk. Let it soak for a few minutes
  2. Add minced meat and eggs and stir the stuffing evenly and evenly
  3. Form small, round meatballs with your hands and place them on a baking tray lined with baking paper
  4. Bake the Swedish meatballs in the oven at 220 degrees for approx. 10 minutes Turn them around as you go
  5. Pour the Swedish meatballs onto a hot, high pan with butter and fry them for a few minutes until golden
  6. Remove the meatballs from the pan and keep the fat in the pan
  7. Add cooking cream and beef broth and heat slowly, stirring frequently with a whisk
  8. Add color and taste with currant jelly, salt and pepper
  9. Smooth the brown sauce with cornstarch
  10. Serve the Swedish meatballs with homemade mashed potatoes and currant jelly
